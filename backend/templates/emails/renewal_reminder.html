Reminder to Renew {{ name }} on Penn Clubs & SAC Fair Signup by 8/24!

Dear {{ name }} leaders,

You are receiving this notification because you were the contact for your organization during the 2019 SAC Activities Fair. You have not yet renewed your club for the {{ year }} school year. If you do not submit a renewal request by August 24th, your club may not appear on the Penn Clubs website in time for new student orientation. Use the green button below to renew your club. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

If you have already graduated, PLEASE FORWARD THIS EMAIL to the current leadership of the group so that they may complete the registration process and sign up for the SAC Fair. If they are not currently listed as an officer for your club on Penn Clubs, they will need to email contact@pennclubs.com with their name, PennKey, and club name.

We have opened the student group registration process for the 2020-2021 academic year. To be considered an active student group on campus, the University requires all student organizations to be registered with and approved by the Office of Student Affairs. This year, OSA has migrated the Student Groups platform from the previous system, G.O. Penn, to Penn Labs' platform, Penn Clubs. All official registry of student groups, including the annual student group registration process, will now be handled through Penn Clubs.

Additionally, the Student Activities Council (SAC) is partnering with Penn Clubs to host a virtual multiday Activities Fair from Sep 1 – 3. More details on the Fair will be shared at a later date by SAC. To participate in the Fair, you must be a student-run club at the University of Pennsylvania approved by the OSA. Registration through the Penn Clubs portal will require PennKey authentication. The deadline to sign up for the SAC Fair is August 24th.

Once the request for registration is approved, the group will be listed on the front page of Penn Clubs and will be searchable for students on the site.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Since this information is being distributed through various listservs and methods, please communicate with your entire executive board before signing up for the fair. ONLY ONE officer of the club should be designated to register the group.

Re-Registration Process (including SAC Fair sign-up) - Deadline Aug. 24th

Again, it is imperative that your group designate ONLY ONE executive board member to complete this process, preferably a member who is listed on pennclubs.com as an officer for your club.

To re-register a returning student group, you can access the Penn Clubs renewal site at https://pennclubs.com/club/pennlabs/renew. This will show you all the clubs of which you are an administrator.

If you are logged in, and do not see any clubs, email contact@pennclubs.com with your PennKey and club name to get access to re-register your club for the 2020-2021 school year.

Follow the 6 steps listed to update your club's information. The final page of the renewal process will ask whether or not your club is interested in participating in the Fall 2020 SAC Fair. To sign up, please simply check the box on this page, and your information will be sent to the SAC Fair Coordinator.

Renew Club & Signup for SAC Fair

If you have any questions or problems regarding re-registration, please send an email to contact@pennclubs.com.

If you have questions specifically regarding the SAC Fair, please send an email to sacfair@sacfunded.net.
