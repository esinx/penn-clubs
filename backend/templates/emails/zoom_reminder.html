[{{ prefix }}] URGENT: Using Zoom for the SAC Fair

Dear {{ name }} officers,

If you are receiving this email, it means your club has linked a non-Zoom link in your SAC Fair Booth.

In order to provide a standardized experience across all clubs for the SAC Fair, we are requesting that all clubs use a Zoom link instead of other services like Campus Groups or Virtual Event. You should also avoid minifying your URL using a shortener service. While other platforms may be used for other schools or events, to provide a more seamless experience for prospective members, OSA and SAC are requesting that all clubs use Zoom.

Please follow the link below for more information on how to use our three-step process to replace your link with a Zoom meeting with all our recommended preferences, how to best take advantage of the tools Penn Clubs has to offer, and tips on how to boost your club to the top of our homepage!

If the officer who created the Zoom link also links their Zoom account to Penn Clubs, we will enable additional features, such as showing the current number of people in your Zoom meeting on the events page.

SAC Fair Guide

If you have any questions about the Penn Clubs platform, please feel free to respond to this email.

If you have any questions about the SAC Fair in general, please contact sacfair@sacfunded.net.
